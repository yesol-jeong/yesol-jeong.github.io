About

Introduction

I am Yesol Jeong, a senior undergraduate student in Chemical and Biological Engineering at Seoul National University. My academic interests center on chemistry, biology, and engineering approaches to biomaterials, metabolism, and regenerative medicine.

Through research internships, I have worked on secretome-based ischemic wound regeneration and artificial C4 photosynthesis in water microdroplets. I also enjoy using programming and AI tools to organize ideas, analyze data, and communicate research clearly.
